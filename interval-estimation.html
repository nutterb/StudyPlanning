2 Interval Estimation

2.1 One Sample T-Interval

2.1.1 Estimation Formula

\[ E = t_{1-\alpha^*, n-1} \cdot \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} \]

  • \(E\) = the margin of error
  • \(\alpha^*\) = the one or two sided significance level (1 - Confidence)
  • \(s\) = the standard deviation
  • \(n\) = the sample size
